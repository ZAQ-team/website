We are pleased to announce that we are an official auditing partner of EverRise. With a plethora of new projects in the DeFi space, it is almost impossible to find safe and secure investment opportunities. We believe that a combination of TechAudit, KYC, and EverOwn can help investors to find such promising projects.

EverOwn empowers developers to build trust within their community while increasing the project's longevity by locking the contract and liquidity and allowing it to be unlocked through community voting when developers need to make changes on the contract, fix issues or apply for exchanges.


Many new projects opt to renounce the contract to establish trust and attract new buyers. The problem with renouncing the contract is that it is permanent. EverOwn offers a solution to build trust within a project’s community while solving centralization risks, without losing the contract ownership forever.

EverOwn fixes this issue by holding and locking the contract within the decentralized application (dApp) on behalf of the community. If something needs to be changed or a problem needs to be fixed, the owner can unlock the contract and get the ownership back with a community vote. Another advantage of transferring ownership to the EverOwn contract is that any new liquidity generated will be auto-assigned to the EverOwn contract.

EverOwn aims to prevent common risks in early-stage projects and provide solutions to benefit developers and investors alike. It has always been our goal to make the DeFi space more secure, and we are therefore delighted to partner with EverRise by recommending EverOwn to our clients.
